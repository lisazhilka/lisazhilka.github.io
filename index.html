Description

The goal is to create an iOS personal finance app SmartBank. Users can top up, receive and send money in different curruncies. Users can create and order new credit/debit cards and use the for payments. Users can create currency accounts, manage them, and view their previous transactions.

The approach to UX will be based oa a Learn-UX methodology where small changes are made upfront and tested with users right away, using low-high fidelity prototypes until an optimal solution is obtained. We’ll focus less on traditional UX deliverables to streamline our work process and achieve business objectives in the least amount of time.
